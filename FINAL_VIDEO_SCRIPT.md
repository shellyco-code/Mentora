## 📋 SCENE 2: TASK (Dashboard) — 30 seconds

**[Visual: Signup → Dashboard loads]**

"The problem is personalization. Everyone gets the same career advice.

My goal: create a system that analyzes your profile, tests your skills, generates a personalized roadmap, and recommends matching jobs. All powered by AI.

I used React and Vite for frontend, Node.js and Express for backend, Firebase for auth and database, and Google Gemini AI for recommendations."

**[Hover over dashboard sections]**

"It identifies strengths, spots skill gaps, and gives recommendations. Real AI understanding, not keyword matching."

4. **Pause briefly** - When switching pages
5. **Emphasize key words** - "AI-powered", "personalized", "adaptive"
